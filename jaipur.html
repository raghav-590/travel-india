Jaipur, Rajasthan

The Pink City — royal forts, vibrant bazaars, and Rajput heritage.

About Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is famously known as the "Pink City" because of the distinctive colour of its buildings. Founded in 1727 by Maharaja Jai Singh II, it was one of India's first planned cities. Jaipur is part of the famous Golden Triangle tourist circuit along with Delhi and Agra. The city is known for its magnificent forts, palaces, temples, and colourful bazaars selling gems, jewellery, textiles, and handicrafts. It is a UNESCO Creative City of Crafts and Folk Art.
