Goa

India's beach paradise — sun, sand, seafood, and endless fun.

About Goa

Goa is India's smallest state by area, located on the western coast. It is famous for its golden beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese colonial heritage. Goa was a Portuguese colony until 1961, and this influence is still visible in its architecture, churches, and cuisine. Whether you want to relax on a quiet beach, explore historic forts, or party at beach clubs, Goa has something for everyone. It is one of the most popular tourist destinations in India and Southeast Asia.
